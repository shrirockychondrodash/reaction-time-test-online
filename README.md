# Reaction Time Test Online 🎯

Test your reaction speed online with this **free, fun, and mobile-friendly reaction time game**! Measure your reflexes, track your best and average times, and challenge your friends to beat your score. Perfect for gamers, athletes, and anyone who wants to improve focus and coordination.

## 🚀 Features

- ✅ Play online directly in your browser  
- ✅ Mobile responsive for phones and tablets  
- ✅ Track best and average reaction times  
- ✅ Viral share buttons: WhatsApp, Facebook, Copy Link  
- ✅ Reset your scores anytime  
- ✅ Monetization-ready with Adsterra ad placements  

## 🎮 How to Play

1. Click on the red box to start.  
2. Wait until it turns green.  
3. Click as fast as you can when the color changes.  
4. See your reaction time in milliseconds!  
5. Share your score with friends and challenge them.
